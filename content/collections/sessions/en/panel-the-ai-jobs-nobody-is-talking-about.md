<p>When people think about AI careers, they often imagine coding and data science. In reality, many of the fastest-growing AI roles sit between technology and real-world application. This session uncovers the hidden jobs that power AI in industry, from system trainers and operators to quality supervisors and decision-makers. It shows young people how AI creates opportunities for a wide range of skills, not just programmers.</p>
