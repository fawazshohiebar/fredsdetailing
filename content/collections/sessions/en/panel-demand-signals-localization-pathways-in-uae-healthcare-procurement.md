<p>The panel aims to strategically align demand, regulation, and manufacturing to accelerate pharmaceutical localization in the UAE. The discussion will focus on identifying priority pharmaceutical products for local production, examining how procurement frameworks can actively incentivize domestic manufacturing, and exploring fast-track regulatory pathways for locally produced medicines. The session will also address structured off-take models, volume commitments, and targeted incentives for API and generics production. Ultimately, the objective is to provide investors and manufacturers with clear visibility on priority molecules, forecasted demand volumes, regulatory expectations, and viable partnership models—creating a transparent and execution-focused roadmap for building a resilient national pharmaceutical ecosystem.</p>
