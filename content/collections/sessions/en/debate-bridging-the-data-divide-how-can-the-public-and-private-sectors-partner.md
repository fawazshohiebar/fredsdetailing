<p>This debate will bring together representatives from both the public and private sectors to discuss the future of data sharing, collaboration, and innovation in the UAE. As the need for timely, trusted, and high-quality data continues to grow, both sectors are becoming increasingly interconnected. The debate will explore how data—when shared responsibly and securely—can support national planning, economic competitiveness, and evidence-based decision-making, while addressing concerns around confidentiality, risk, and business value</p>
