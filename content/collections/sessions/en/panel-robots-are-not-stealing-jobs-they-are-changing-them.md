<p>Automation and robotics are reshaping industry, but they are also creating new roles, skills, and opportunities. This session explores how jobs are evolving as humans and machines work together on the factory floor. Young people will hear how technical, creative, and problem-solving skills are becoming more valuable, and why the future of manufacturing depends on people who can work alongside intelligent systems, not compete with them.</p>
