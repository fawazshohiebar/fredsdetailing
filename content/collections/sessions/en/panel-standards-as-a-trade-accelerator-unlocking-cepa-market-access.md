<p>The panel will highlight how standards act as a catalyst for market access and trade facilitation within the framework of Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreements (CEPA). By aligning national standards with international best practices, countries can reduce technical barriers to trade, strengthen mutual recognition, and open new opportunities for exporters and SMEs to access global markets.</p>
