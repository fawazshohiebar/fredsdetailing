<p>This session focuses on strategies for scaling advanced manufacturing technologies beyond pilot projects; the challenge is no longer proving what advanced manufacturing can achieve in leading factories, but scaling those capabilities across the entire industrial ecosystem. Participants learn how to replicate successful innovations across sectors, creating widespread efficiency gains, resilience, and competitive advantage.</p>
