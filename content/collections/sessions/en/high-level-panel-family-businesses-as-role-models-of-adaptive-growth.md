<p>In 2026’s Year of the Family, the panel will explore how UAE family enterprises, responsible for roughly 60 % of national GDP and over 80 % of private-sector employment, are navigating geopolitical tensions, diversified trade partnerships, and evolving investment flows to sustain long‐term competitiveness and cross‐border influence. This is especially relevant as global economic leadership becomes increasingly multipolar under continuous US–China competition, shifting supply chains, and geopolitical fragmentation, and family businesses in the UAE are proving to be uniquely positioned to serve as stabilizing engines of resilient growth.</p>
