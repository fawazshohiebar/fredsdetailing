<p>Designing a product is only the beginning. Scaling it, manufacturing it, and exporting it globally is where real impact is created. This session explains how the UAE provides a platform for young innovators to build products locally and compete internationally. It explores what it takes to design with global markets in mind and how youth can think beyond borders from the very start.</p>
