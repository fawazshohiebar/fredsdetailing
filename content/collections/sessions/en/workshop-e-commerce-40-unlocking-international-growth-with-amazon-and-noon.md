<p>This workshop brings together leading e-commerce platforms Amazon and Noon to guide companies—particularly SMEs—on how to successfully list, market, and sell their products online while expanding their reach to regional and international markets. Industry experts from both platforms will share practical insights on marketplace entry, product onboarding, pricing strategies, logistics, digital marketing, and compliance requirements for cross-border trade. </p><p>Participants will gain an understanding of best practices for building a strong online presence, optimizing product visibility, managing fulfillment and delivery, and leveraging platform tools such as analytics, advertising, and seller support services. The session will also explore potential areas of collaboration between businesses and these platforms, including export opportunities, partnerships, and programs designed to support SME digital transformation.</p>
